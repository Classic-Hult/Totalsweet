Vegan cakes and bakes

It's fair to say we've got a slight bias towards plant based foods, given Total Sweet Xylitol is made from sustainable birch and beech wood, and we've worked with some incredible vegan chefs over the years.

Below we've listed some of our favourite vegan recipes, which use Total Sweet Xylitol. 

In general you don't need any special recipes or measures to use xylitol. Simply replace sugar with it one for one in vegan recipes and voila your favourite vegan cakes and bakes, but with less sugar - win win all round. 

We hope you enjoy!
