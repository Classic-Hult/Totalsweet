Total Sweet lets you enjoy the taste without the guilt

We all want to lose weight, get fitter or have a better diet, but it is often far easier said than done.

Total Sweet xylitol makes it that little bit easier for you by allowing you to enjoy sweet foods and drink without the guilt of consuming too much sugar.

At Healthy by Nature we believe the key to staying healthy is eating sensibly and taking regular exercise. We also believe that when dieting, it is important not to sacrifice other areas of your health by eating artificial and bland foods, which is why Total Sweet is so special, being both 100% natural and tasty.

Total Sweet's benefits aren't all about diet however and it also offers a great way to actively help with dental health, going beyond simply cutting sugar out of your diet.
