About Us

Total Sweet is a real health product

ECW Capital is a distributor of Total Sweet products in Ghana, which was formed by ECW Capital in 2005 by a small group of health-conscious friends (in the United Kingdom) who began to feel concerned about the amount of so called “health products” emerging on the market.

We'd  had enough of seeing the phrase “health food” exploited to sell poor, undifferentiated products for large profits and wanted to offer something different. .

Our philosophy is that outstanding, innovative and high quality products with real benefits drive success themselves, without the need for gimmicky marketing, and believe Total Sweet fulfills all of those criteria.


Born from a belief that true health products should:

  • Have no hidden secrets
  • Not pretend to be something they’re not
  • Have easy to read labels
  • Have real, tested and proven health benefits
  • Not compromise quality or taste for those benefits
